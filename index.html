About Me

I recently switched careers into web development after a long career of victim advocacy. I found my passion for coding and cybersecurity after working with victims of human trafficking. I learned about groups like AnnieCannons that work with trafficking survivors to help them develop skills in software engingeering, front-end and back-end development, and other tech fields.

I realized that I wanted to follow a similar path and bring a similar program to my homestate of Virginia. Most recently, I have completed bootcamps with Front-End Foxes and Memorisely to better my skills. I hope to one day one work a company that empowers women and other marginalized groups by teaching them coding and assisting them with breaking into tech!

My passion is making technology more accessible for others as others have done for me.
